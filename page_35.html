341. Overosaurus

  • This dwarf titanosaur was announced to the world in 2013.

342. Oviraptor

  • Turns out that this “egg thief” got a bad rap.

343. Oxalaia

  • This spinosaur was recently discovered in Brazil.

344. Ozraptor

  • Not much is known about this Australian theropod.

  • This plant-eater gave new meaning to the word “blockhead.”

  • This “thick-nosed lizard” roamed the North American forests.

347. Palaeoscincus

  • This “ancient skink” was actually an armored dinosaur.

348. Pamparaptor

  • This raptor was discovered in the Argentinian Pampas.

349. Panamericansaurus

  • This titanosaur was named after an energy company.

350. Panphagia

  • Its name is Greek for “eats everything.”
