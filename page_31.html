Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 31/50

301. Metriacanthosaurus

  • Yet another dinosaur that was once mistaken for Megalosaurus.

302. Microceratops

  • Probably the smallest ceratopsian that ever lived.

303. Micropachycephalosaurus

  • The current record-holder for the longest dinosaur name.

304. Microraptor

  • This tiny feathered dinosaur had four wings rather than two.

305. Minmi

  • An early (and very dumb) ankylosaur from Australia.

306. Minotaurasaurus

  • Named after the half-man, half-bull of Greek myth.

307. Miragaia

  • This stegosaur had an unusually long neck.

308. Mirischia

  • Its name means “wonderful pelvis.”

309. Mochlodon

  • One of the few dinosaurs ever to be discovered in Austria.

310. Mojoceratops

  • This ceratopsian had a heart-shaped frill.
