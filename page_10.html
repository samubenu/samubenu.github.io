Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 10/50

91. Ceratonykus

  • This dino-bird was discovered in Mongolia in 2009.

92. Cetiosauriscus

  • Not to be confused with the more famous Cetiosaurus.

93. Changyuraptor

  • Was this feathered dinosaur capable of flight?

94. Chasmosaurus

  • The only dinosaur that came with its own awning.

95. Chilantaisaurus

  • This large theropod may have been ancestral to Spinosaurus.

96. Chindesaurus

  • This early dinosaur was a close relative of Herrerasaurus.

97. Chubutisaurus

  • This titanosaur was on Tyrannotitan’s lunch menu.

98. Chungkingosaurus

  • This early stegosaur had some primitive characteristics.

99. Citipati

  • This Mongolian theropod was a close relative of Oviraptor.

100. Coahuilaceratops

  • It had the longest horns of any known ceratopsian dinosaur.
