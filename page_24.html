Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 24/50

231. Jainosaurus

  • Named after the Indian paleontologist Sohan Lal Jain.

232. Jaxartosaurus

  • A poorly known hadrosaur from central Asia.

233. Jeholosaurus

  • This ornithopod may have had an omnivorous diet.

234. Jianchangosaurus

  • One of the earliest therizinosaurs in the fossil record.

235. Jinfengopteryx

  • This feathered dinosaur was once thought to be a true bird.

236. Jingshanosaurus

  • A close relative of Yunnanosaurus.

237. Jinzhousaurus

  • This Asian dinosaur was one of the first hadrosaurs.

238. Jobaria

  • A strange, short-tailed African sauropod.

239. Judiceratops

  • The earliest Chasmosaurus ancestor yet identified.

240. Juratyrant

  • This early tyrannosaur was discovered in England.
