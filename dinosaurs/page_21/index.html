209. Giraffatitan

Brief Description: - Might this “giant giraffe” have been a species of Brachiosaurus?

Additional Information: Giraffatitan (name meaning “titanic giraffe”) is a genus of sauropod dinosaur that lived during the late Jurassic Period (Kimmeridgian-Tithonian stages) in what is now Lindi Region, Tanzania.Only one species is known, G. brancai, named in honor of German paleontologist Wilhelm von Branca, who was a driving force behind the expedition that discovered it in the Tendaguru Formation.
