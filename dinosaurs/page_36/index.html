Brief Description: - A bizarre therizinosaur from Asia.

360. Neimongosaurus

Brief Description: - A rare therizinosaur from inner Mongolia.

Additional Information: Neimongosaurus (meaning “Nei Mongol lizard”) is a genus of herbivorous therizinosaur theropod dinosaur that lived in China during the Late Cretaceous period. Its fossils are known from the strata of the Iren Dabasu Formation.It is known from two specimens, discovered in 1999 by researchers from the Ministry of Land and Resources and described two years later.
