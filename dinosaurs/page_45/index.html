444. Saltasaurus

Brief Description: - The first armored sauropod ever to be discovered.

Additional Information: Saltasaurus (which means “lizard from Salta”) is a genus of saltasaurid dinosaur of the Late Cretaceous period of Argentina.Small among sauropods, though still heavy by the standards of modern creatures, Saltasaurus was characterized by a short neck and stubby limbs. It was the first genus of sauropod known to possess armour of bony plates embedded in its skin.

446. Sanjuansaurus

Brief Description: - An early theropod from South America.

Additional Information: Life restoration. Sanjuansaurus was comparable in size to a medium-sized Herrerasaurus, with a thigh bone that was 395 millimetres (15.6 in) long and a tibia that is 360 millimetres (14 in) in length (in the description paper the tibia is listed with 260 mm in length due to a typo).
