257. Isanosaurus

Brief Description: - One of the first sauropods ever to walk the earth.

Additional Information: The Isanosaurus, which lived approximately 210 million years ago, holds the title of one of the oldest known sauropod dinosaurs. Discovered in Thailand, this herbivorous giant offers a crucial glimpse into the early existence of sauropods, a group renowned for their massive size and long necks.

259. Jainosaurus

Brief Description: - Named after the Indian paleontologist Sohan Lal Jain.

Additional Information: Jainosaurus is a genus of titanosaurian sauropod dinosaur of India and wider Asia, which lived in the Maastrichtian (approximately 68 million years ago).It is thought to have been about the same size as its contemporary relative Isisaurus, measuring 18 metres (59 ft) long and weighing 15 metric tons (17 short tons).
