Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 15/50

141. Edmontonia

  • This armored dinosaur never actually lived in Edmonton.

142. Edmontosaurus

  • This large, duck-billed herbivore was a contemporary of T. Rex.

143. Efraasia

  • This Triassic herbivore may have been ancestral to sauropods.

144. Einiosaurus

  • This ceratopsian was a close relative of Centrosaurus.

145. Ekrixinatosaurus

  • Its name means “explosion-born lizard.”

146. Elaphrosaurus

  • A lightweight theropod from the late Jurassic.

147. Elmisaurus

  • This “foot lizard” was a close relative of Oviraptor.

148. Elrhazosaurus

  • Once classified as a species of Valdosaurus.

149. Enigmosaurus

  • This “puzzle lizard” was closely related to Therizinosaurus.

150. Eoabelisaurus

  • The earliest abelisaurid theropod yet identified.
