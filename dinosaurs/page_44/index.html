431. Suzhousaurus

  • A large, early Cretaceous therizinosaur.

432. Tachiraptor

  • The first meat-eating dinosaur ever to be discovered in Venezuela.

433. Talarurus

  • This ankylosaur was discovered in the Gobi Desert.

434. Talenkauen

A rare ornithopod from South America.

435. Talos

  • This dinosaur was found with an injured big toe.

436. Tanius

  • Not much is known about this Chinese hadrosaur.

437. Tanycolagreus

  • This mysterious theropod was once thought to be a species of Coelurus.

438. Taohelong

  • The first “polacanthine” ankylosaur ever to be discovered in Asia.

439. Tapuiasaurus

  • A recently discovered titanosaur from South America.

440. Tarascosaurus

  • The only known abelisaur of the northern hemisphere.
