56. Argentinosaurus

Brief Description: - Possibly the largest dinosaur that ever lived.

Additional Information: Argentinosaurus (meaning “lizard from Argentina”) is a genus of giant sauropod dinosaur that lived during the Late Cretaceous period in what is now Argentina.Although it is only known from fragmentary remains, Argentinosaurus is one of the largest known land animals of all time, perhaps the largest, measuring 30-35 m (98-115 ft) long and weighing 65-80 t (72-88 short tons).
