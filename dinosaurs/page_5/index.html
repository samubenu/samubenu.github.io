Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 5/50

41. Aralosaurus

  • Not much is known about this central Asian duck-billed dinosaur.

42. Archaeopteryx

  • This ancient dino-bird was about the size of a modern pigeon.

43. Archaeornithomimus

  • A likely ancestor of Ornithomimus.

44. Arcovenator

  • This fierce abelisaur was recently discovered in France.

45. Arcusaurus

  • This prosauropod was recently discovered in South Africa.

46. Argentinosaurus

  • Possibly the largest dinosaur that ever lived.

47. Argyrosaurus

  • A plus-sized titanosaur from South America.

48. Aristosuchus

  • This “noble crocodile” was actually a dinosaur.

49. Arrhinoceratops

  • This ceratopsian was named for its “missing” nose horn.

50. Astrodon

  • The official state dinosaur of Maryland.
