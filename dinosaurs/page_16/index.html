Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 16/50

151. Eobrontosaurus

  • This “dawn brontosaurus” isn’t accepted by most experts.

152. Eocarcharia

  • This “dawn shark” prowled the woodlands of northern Africa.

153. Eocursor

  • This late Triassic reptile was one of the earliest true dinosaurs.

154. Eodromaeus

  • Yet another ancient theropod from South America.

155. Eolambia

  • An early hadrosaur from North America.

156. Eoraptor

  • This tiny dinosaur was among the first of its kind.

157. Eotriceratops

  • This “dawn Triceratops” was recently discovered in Canada.

158. Eotyrannus

  • This early tyrannosaur looked more like a raptor.

159. Epachthosaurus

  • This “heavy lizard” was relatively primitive for its time and place.

160. Equijubus

  • Its name is Greek for “horse mane.”
