Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 47/50

461. Uberabatitan

  • Discovered in the Uberaba region of Brazil.

462. Udanoceratops

  • The largest ceratopsian to run on two legs.

463. Unaysaurus

  • One of the oldest prosauropods yet discovered.

464. Unescoceratops

  • Named after the United Nation’s UNESCO.

465. Urbacodon

  • This Troodon-like predator was discovered in Uzbekistan.

466. Uteodon

  • It was once classified as a species of Camptosaurus.

467. Vagaceratops

  • This big-frilled dinosaur was closely related to Kosmoceratops.

468. Vahiny

  • Its name is Malagasy for “traveler.”

469. Valdoraptor

  • This early “bird mimic” dinosaur lived in England.

470. Valdosaurus

  • This ornithopod was discovered on the Isle of Wight.
