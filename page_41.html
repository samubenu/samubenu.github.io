Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 41/50

401. Secernosaurus

  • The first hadrosaur to be discovered in South America.

402. Seismosaurus

  • It was huge, to be sure, but might it have been a species of Diplodocus?

403. Seitaad

  • This small dinosaur may have been buried in an avalanche.

404. Sellosaurus

  • Another early prosauropod of the Triassic period.

405. Shamosaurus

  • This Mongolian ankylosaur was a close relative of Gobisaurus.

406. Shanag

  • A basal raptor of early Cretaceous Asia.

407. Shenzhousaurus

  • A small, primitive ornithomimid from China.

408. Siamodon

  • This ornithopod was recently discovered in Thailand.

409. Siamosaurus

  • This may (or may not) have been a spinosaur from Thailand.

410. Siamotyrannus

  • Despite its name, it wasn’t a true tyrannosaur.
