Dinosaur Encyclopedia - Page 50/50

491. Yinlong

  • This “hidden dragon” was an early ceratopsian.

492. Yongjinglong

  • This titanosaur was recently discovered in China.

493. Yunnanosaurus

  • One of the last prosauropods to walk the earth.

494. Yutyrannus

  • The largest feathered tyrannosaur yet identified.

495. Zby

  • This dinosaur’s name was inversely proportional to its size.

496. Zhenyuanlong

  • Also known as the “fluffy feathered poodle from hell.”

497. Zhongyuansaurus

  • The only known ankylosaur to lack a tail club.

498. Zhuchengosaurus

  • This hadrosaur was even bigger than Shantungosaurus.

499. Zuolong

  • It was named after General Tso, of Chinese restaurant fame.

500. Zupaysaurus

  • This “devil lizard” was one of the earliest theropods.
