31. Angulomastacator

  • This dinosaur had a strangely shaped upper jaw.

32. Animantarx

  • This “living fortress” was discovered in an unusual way.

33. Ankylosaurus

  • This dinosaur was the Cretaceous equivalent of a Sherman tank.

34. Anodontosaurus

  • This “toothless lizard” actually had a full set of choppers.

35. Anserimimus

  • This “goose mimic” didn’t bear much of a resemblance.

36. Antarctopelta

  • The first dinosaur fossil ever discovered in Antarctica.

37. Antarctosaurus

  • This titanosaur may or may not have lived in Antarctica.

38. Antetonitrus

  • Either a very late prosauropod or a very early sauropod.

39. Apatosaurus

  • The dinosaur formerly known as Brontosaurus.

40. Aquilops

  • The earliest ceratopsian ever to be discovered in North America.
