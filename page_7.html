  • This “grandmother tyrant” was one of the first tyrannosaurs.

  • One of the earliest of the duck-billed dinosaurs.

  • A small ceratopsian from central Asia.

  • No one is quite sure how to classify this theropod.

  • Yes, this tiny raptor was named after you-know-who.

  • An enormous plant-eater with a tiny head.

68. Barsboldia

  • This hadrosaur was named after Rinchen Barsbold.

69. Baryonyx

  • You wouldn’t want to clip this dinosaur’s claws.

70. Batyrosaurus

  • One of the most basal hadrosaurs yet identified.
